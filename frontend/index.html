FAQ

Common questions.

What does MCP mean in this product?

MCP refers to the product framework and workflow layer that helps organize stock and currency research into a more consistent, reusable process.

Is this for active trading or research support?

This product is positioned for research support, market monitoring, and analysis workflows. It is not a guarantee of outcomes and should not be treated as a substitute for professional financial advice.

Can teams collaborate inside the platform?

Yes. Team plans are designed for shared workflows, consistent research templates, and easier collaboration across analysts and stakeholders.

Does it cover both stocks and currencies?

Yes. The product is designed specifically around U.S. stock research and currency monitoring so users can evaluate both company-level and broader market context together.

Is the information financial advice?

No. The platform provides tools and research support only. Users should evaluate all information independently and consult qualified professionals where appropriate.
